# Routes #
/api/v3/extraction_forms_projects_sections/{extraction_forms_projects_section_number}
/api/v3/extraction_forms_projects/{extraction_forms_project_number}/extraction_forms_projects_sections

# Including Elements #
- arms suggestion
- outcomes suggestion
- risk of bias questionnaire
- questions designed for extraction

# FHIR Version #
4.3.0

# Description #
Extraction form contains four possible elements. Arm and outcome suggestion are stored as a FHIR EvidenceVariable resource. Risk of bias questionnaire and questions designed for extraction are stored as a FHIR Questionnaire resource. It can be obtained by either extraction form number or extraction form section number. Both of them can be observed in srdrplus website. Extraction form equals to extraction_forms_projects, and extraction form section equals to extraction_forms_projects_sections. Route with extraction form section number will give you either a FHIR EvidenceVariable resource or a FHIR Questionnaire resource, another will give you a FHIR Bundle resource containing all extraction form sections in your extraction form as entries.
